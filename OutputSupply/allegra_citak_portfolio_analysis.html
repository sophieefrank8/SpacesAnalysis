Key Definitions

  • D2L (Direct to Landlord): Spaces where Tandem is working directly with the building owner/landlord. This is Allegra's primary coverage area in New York.
  • Public / Live space: status = 'PUBLISHED' AND isSearchable = true — visible on the public Tandem website and map.
  • firstSearchableDate: The timestamp when a space was first made publicly searchable. Used as the "went live" date throughout this report.
  • Live deal: contractSignedAt IS NOT NULL AND proposedStartDate <= today AND (moveOutDate IS NULL OR moveOutDate > today) AND match status is active.
  • Fee normalization: One-time-for-term fees (FEE_ONE_TIME_FOR_TERM) are divided by lease term length to produce a comparable monthly figure.
  • Opportunity portfolio: All spaces linked to opportunities owned by Allegra in the opportunity_space junction table — represents her full sourcing and outreach coverage beyond just NY D2L.
