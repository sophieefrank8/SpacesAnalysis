Guest Policy

By visiting Basecamp, all guests agree to the following terms.

  • 1. Stay with your host Guests must remain with their host at all times and may not access areas beyond what their host is authorized to use.
  • 2. Do not use unrented desks or equipment Only sit at or use desks, monitors, and equipment that belong to your host. Do not occupy desks rented by other tenants.
  • 3. Do not touch others' belongings Respect the property of all tenants. Do not handle, move, or disturb items that do not belong to your host.
  • 4. Keep noise to a minimum Use hushed voices in open work areas. Take calls and louder conversations to designated phone booths or meeting rooms.
  • 5. Leave common areas as you found them Clean up after yourself in kitchens, lounges, and shared spaces. Dispose of waste properly and return any items you use.
  • 6. No property damage Any damage to Basecamp property or the belongings of tenants is the responsibility of the guest and their host.
  • 7. Respect restricted areas Do not enter server rooms, storage areas, or any space marked as staff- or tenant-only without explicit authorization.
  • 8. Photography and filming Do not photograph or film other tenants, their work, or screens without their consent.

By confirming your visit you also agree that Basecamp and Tandem may occasionally send you information about events, membership, and updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Violations of this policy may result in removal from the premises. Basecamp reserves the right to update these terms at any time.
