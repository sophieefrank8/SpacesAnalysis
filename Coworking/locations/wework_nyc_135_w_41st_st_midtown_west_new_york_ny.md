## Building Features

This WeWork location offers a variety of office solutions, including open coworking areas, private offices for teams of various sizes, and dedicated desks, complemented by bookable meeting rooms and flexible event spaces. Members have access to numerous shared amenities such as relaxed lounges, phone booths, high-speed Wi-Fi, and an onsite community team. Notable building features include ten shared and private terraces with city views, Bar16—an in-house bar and pizzeria on the 16th floor, a dedicated auditorium, a recording studio, and a food hall, all within a seventeen-floor Grade A building.
