WeWork at One Beacon Street provides flexible workspaces, including private offices for teams of 1 to 100+ members, full-floor options, and various coworking solutions, all set within a classic 34-story Class A Boston skyscraper. The building itself boasts impressive 360-degree skyline views, on-site parking, bike storage, a fitness center, a lobby café, and dedicated event spaces.
