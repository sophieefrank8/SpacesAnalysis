## Building Features

WeWork at Dock 72 offers a modern workspace within a 2019-built Class A building, featuring private terraces, 360-degree waterfront views, and 14-foot ceilings. Members can choose from private offices for teams of various sizes, dedicated desks, or flexible coworking options. Shared amenities include bright lounges, fully stocked kitchens, meeting rooms, phone booths, a wellness room, dog-friendly areas, outdoor spaces, recreational games, a bike storage, and an onsite food hall.
