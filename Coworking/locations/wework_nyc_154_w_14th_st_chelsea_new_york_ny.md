## Building Features

This WeWork location offers diverse office solutions, including open coworking areas, private offices for teams ranging from 1 to over 100, full-floor options, and dedicated desks, alongside versatile meeting and event spaces bookable by the hour. Members enjoy a wealth of shared amenities such as relaxed lounges, ten shared and private terraces with sweeping city views, a barista, a food hall, recreational games, and wellness and parent's rooms. Housed in a Grade A building across seventeen floors, it also features a dedicated auditorium, dog-friendly spaces, showers, and comprehensive tech services with onsite staff.
