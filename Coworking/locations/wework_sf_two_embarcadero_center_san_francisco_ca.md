## Building Features

This WeWork location at Two Embarcadero Center provides a flexible range of office solutions, including private offices for teams of 1 to 100+ members, dedicated desks, and coworking day passes, all spread across four mid-level and two top floors with sweeping Bay and bridge views. Members have access to shared amenities like equipped meeting rooms, comfortable lounges, private phone booths, and stocked kitchens with complimentary refreshments, along with secure Wi-Fi and unique features such as a parent's room, wellness room, bike storage, and EV charging stations. The building also offers convenient on-site dining and retail options within its open-air shopping center.
