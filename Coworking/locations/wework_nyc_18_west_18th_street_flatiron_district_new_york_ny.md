## Building Features

WeWork at 18 West 18th Street provides flexible workspace across five floors of a charming 1902 heritage building, featuring private offices for teams of all sizes, full-floor options, and day passes. Members can enjoy high-speed Wi-Fi, fully stocked kitchens, common areas, phone booths, and meeting rooms. Unique amenities include a dedicated classroom and a new parent's room, all within a dog-friendly environment supported by onsite staff and original architectural details.
